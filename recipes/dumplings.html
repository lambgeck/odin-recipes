Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings

The perfect weeknight family pleaser.

If you want to use this as a pot pie filling, decrease the chicken broth to 2 cups.
If you have a mini food processor, it's perfect for step 5. Transfer the flour-butter mixture to a bowl before proceeding.

    Ingredients:
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced carrot
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thigs
  • 1 pound bone-in chicken breast
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
  • and blah blah blah...

    Directions:
  1. Pour the olive oil into the instant pot, on the saute function.
  2. Add chicken broth, chicken thighs and all that stuff into the pot.
  3. Release pressure carefullly using the something something lol
  4. Mash butter with flour
  5. Combine flour, baking powder, and other shit together.
  6. Shred cooled chicken and return to the instant pot.
